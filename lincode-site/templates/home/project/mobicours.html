MobiCours

Subject

MobiCours is an iphone web application for students of Telecom Paris.
You can consult your lesson schedule if you have a real login of Telecom Paris. Or you can try the demo.
Visit the URL: http://mobicours.appspot.com/.



Web Site

You can visit the MobiCours via Mobile Sarafi on iphone, Mobile Chrome on android, or on PC.

I use Google App Engine as the server. On the client side, Mobicours is powered by jQtouch.

How to use

You can visit http://mobicours.appspot.com/ by your iphone safari, than add this page to the main screen.
